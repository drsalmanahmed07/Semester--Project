Our Master Chef

Fatima Ali

Fatima Ali is a Pakistani-American executive chef, restaurateur and television personality. She is known for her successful appearances on reality cooking shows Chopped and Top Chef, and for winning a James Beard Foundation Award for her essay on living with sarcoma.

Zubaida Tariq Ilyas

Zubaida Tariq Ilyas is a Pakistani chef, herbalist, and cooking expert. She was the first celebrity cook of Pakistan, appearing on numerous TV shows, and was also known for her lifehacks. She has won multiple awards on national and international level.

Ammara Noman

Ammara Noman is a Pakistani chef who rose to prominence after winning the title of first MasterChef Pakistan. She beat about 100 contestants and her 15 co-contestants who entered the Masterchef Kitchen. She has won multiple awards on national and international level.

Zarnak Sidhwa

Zarnak Sidhwa (born 15 December 1972) is a Pakistani chef and chocolatier.[2] She is featured in Hum Masala TV show Food Diaries, and used to be a part of Chocoholics. She belongs to Parsi community of Karachi. She is well known for his mind blowing cooking.
